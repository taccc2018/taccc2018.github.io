Brief about TAC CC

We are a large club, fielding 3 teams on most Saturdays. We have teams playing in the lowest to the highest Cambridgeshire Cricket League. You will find a suitable, compatible level spot for playing. We also field teams for mid-week games and Sunday games. 1st XI plays in the senior level 2 and 2nd XI plays in the Junior League 1 South with Reach as home ground for both teams. 3rd XI plays in the Junior League 3 North with Balsham as home ground
